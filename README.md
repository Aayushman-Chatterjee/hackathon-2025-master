# Portfolio Risk Assessment App

A financial app aimed at teaching new investors how to craft their portfolio based on their **risk** and **earning appetite**.

Users are classified as **Safe**, **Balanced**, or **Risky** investors based on their current income, assets, and lifestyle. They can reveal their day-to-day expenses and receive an overall score showing which financial decisions bring them closer to their ideal investment strategy and which move them further away.

The app also provides insights into stocks, loans, assets, and overall portfolio management.
